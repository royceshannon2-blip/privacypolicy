Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how our internal-use application (the "App") collects, uses, and
handles information when integrated with Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram).

Internal Use Declaration: This App is developed and maintained exclusively for private
business operations. It is not intended for public use, commercial distribution, or use by third
parties.

2. Purpose of Data Processing
The primary purpose of this App is to improve and optimize our business's internal usage and
presence on Facebook and Instagram. Data accessed via Meta APIs is used solely for:
Analyzing internal business page performance and engagement metrics.
Streamlining the management of our business content and communications.
Improving our marketing strategies and operational efficiency on Meta platforms.
Automating internal workflows related to our official business accounts.
3. Information We Access
Through the Facebook and Instagram Graph APIs, the App may access:
Public Profile Information: Basic account details associated with our business pages.
Insights & Analytics: Aggregated data regarding post reach, impressions, and engagement.
Content Data: Posts, comments, and media published by our business accounts.
We only request the minimum permissions necessary to fulfill our internal business objectives.
4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any data obtained through
the App to outside parties. Because the App is for internal use only, data remains within our
controlled business environment.

The security of our business data is important to us. We implement industry-standard

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our internal processes or
